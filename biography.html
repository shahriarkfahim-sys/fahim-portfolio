Biography

Speech Therapist · Social Entrepreneur · Development Worker · Disability Activist

Photograph
forthcoming

Md. Shahriar Kabir Fahim's journey is woven with threads of disability advocacy, youth mobilisation, social development, and community connection. A Speech Therapist turned Social Entrepreneur, Development Worker, and Disability Activist, he has worked in the areas of partnership, facilitation, and speech-digital communication — empowering communities and designing inclusive systems.

He holds a Master of Social Science in Communication Disorders from the University of Dhaka (CGPA 3.62) and currently serves as Deputy Manager at the Social Innovation Lab (SIL), BRAC. He has worked as Senior Officer at BRAC's Youth Platform and as a Speech and Language Therapist at Sunrise Autism Care and BSMMU.

Driven by a solution-centric mindset, he co-founded Connecting Disorders — a social venture empowering individuals with disabilities — and established the Amrai Bangladesh Team, a volunteer force for positive activism. His work has been recognised by BRAC and UNDP. His personal motto: "I never lose; either I win or learn."
