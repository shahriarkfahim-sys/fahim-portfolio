## Biography

Shahriar K Fahim's journey is woven with threads of disability advocacy, youth mobilization, social development, and community connection. He has worked in the areas of partnership, facilitation, and speech-digital communication, empowering communities and designing inclusive systems.

After graduating with a merit scholarship and a vibrant extracurricular life at **Dhaka University**, he began his career at **BRAC Youth Platform**, where he mobilized youth to create impactful community projects and foster social engagement.

As a **Speech Therapist**, he has worked with public and private health clinics, including **BSMMU (Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University)**, to transform communication challenges into opportunities.

Driven by a solution-centric mindset, he co-founded **Connecting Disorders**, a social venture that empowers individuals with disabilities through innovative solutions. He also established the **'Amrai Bangladesh Team'**, a volunteer force dedicated to positive change.

His work has been recognized by organizations like **BRAC** and **UNDP**.
