About

Speech Therapist turned Social Entrepreneur, Development Worker, and Disability Activist.

Md. Shahriar Kabir Fahim works at the intersection of communication disorders, disability advocacy, and social development in Bangladesh. Deputy Manager at BRAC's Social Innovation Lab, co-founder of Connecting Disorders, and recognised by BRAC and UNDP for youth-led social change.
