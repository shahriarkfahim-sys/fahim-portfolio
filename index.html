About

Speech Therapist turned Social Entrepreneur, Development Worker, and Disability Activist.

Md. Shahriar Kabir Fahim works at the intersection of social development, disability advocacy, and entrepreneurship in Bangladesh. Currently, he is working as a Deputy Manager at BRAC's Social Innovation Lab, co-founder of Connecting Disorders and Jotner Dokan.
